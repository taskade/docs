How do I purchase Taskade Pro in the iOS App or Apple App Store?
----------------------------------------------------------------

1.   Open [Taskade for iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/taskade-team-agenda-chats/id1264713923) and navigate to your account settings. 
2.   Tap upgrade to Taskade Unlimited.

How do I cancel Taskade Pro subscription in the App Store?
----------------------------------------------------------

1.   Launch the Settings app in iPhone or iPad. 
2.   Tap iTunes & App Store. 
3.   Tap on your Apple ID. 
4.   Tap View Apple ID when the pop up window appears. 
5.   Enter your Apple ID password or touch/face ID when prompted. 
6.   Tap Subscriptions. 
7.   Tap the Subscription you want to cancel.
