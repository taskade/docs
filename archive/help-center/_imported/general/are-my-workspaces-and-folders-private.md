**Overview**
============

All Taskade [Workspaces](https://intercom.help/taskade/en/articles/8958483) and [Teams](https://help.taskade.com/en/articles/8958495-create-a-folder-subspace) are private by default. Other users can only access your spaces if they are invited by you or another space member.

**üí° Note**: Teams can be [set to public](https://intercom.help/taskade/en/articles/8958493) for workspace members by selecting permission when creating or editing the team. This way, members can have instant access to public teams. Visit [Team Permissions](https://intercom.help/taskade/en/articles/8958493) to learn more.
