**What Is Quick Add?**
----------------------

The Quick Add widget is a powerful companion tool that lives in your OS Tray/Menu bar. The widget is part of [Taskade desktop apps](https://www.taskade.com/downloads) and lets you quickly add tasks to Projects without opening them in the app. You can even select a target [Block](https://intercom.help/taskade/en/articles/8958528) for each task.
