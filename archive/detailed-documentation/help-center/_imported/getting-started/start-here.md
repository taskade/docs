**How To Create A New Project**
-------------------------------

You can üìù **[Create a Project](https://help.taskade.com/en/articles/8958370-create-a-project)** for just about anything. Keep track of tasks, start a DIY project, or collaborate and **[communicate](https://help.taskade.com/en/articles/8958419-start-a-video-call)** with your team on the same page.
