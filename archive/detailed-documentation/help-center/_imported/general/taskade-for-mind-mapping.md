**Overview**
------------

If you've ever felt like regular lists just don't cut, you're going to love mind maps. Mind mapping is a powerful technique for capturing ideas in a visual format. Unlike linear note-taking, mind mapping mirrors the way our brains think ‚Äî jumping from idea to idea and making connections. Here's how to start mind mapping with Taskade.
