Access projects that have been shared with you for easy collaboration.

Updated over a year ago

**Overview**
------------

Taskade users can [invite you to their projects](https://intercom.help/taskade/en/articles/8958373) even if you're not a member of their workspace. The **Shared with me**menu lets you access those projects in one place.

**Shared Folders**
------------------

**Shared with me** also shows all Folders you're invited to. You **don't own shared Folders** but you can still edit or view them depending on your [level of access](https://intercom.help/taskade/en/articles/8958434).
