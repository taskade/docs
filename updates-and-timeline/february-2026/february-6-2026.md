### Space Icon -> Favicons

App favicons are now based on your space icons. Choose an emoji or upload an image as your [space icon](https://help.taskade.com/en/articles/8958485-edit-a-workspace-app) and watch it show up as your app's favicon.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/CleanShot 2026-02-04 at 16.15.06@2x (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
Do remember to republish your app after changing your space icon to see your new favicon apply.
{% endhint %}
