## What's New:

### Docs (Beta) and List View in Production

Docs (Beta) and List View have shipped to production. Docs gives you a long-form writing surface inside Taskade projects, while List View offers a streamlined linear display of your tasks alongside the existing view options.

***

### Inbound Webhooks for Pro+ Plans

Inbound webhooks are now available on all Pro and above plans. Use them to trigger automations from external systems by sending an HTTP request to a Taskade-generated endpoint. [Webhooks](../../../apis-living-system-development/webhooks.md)

***

### MCP Access on All Paid Plans

MCP (Model Context Protocol) access is now included on all paid plans. Free users can connect an MCP server, but workspace-scoped actions such as `list_spaces` require a paid plan. [Workspace MCP](../../../apis-living-system-development/workspace-mcp.md)

***

### Service CRM Genesis Starter

A new "Service CRM" starter template is available in Genesis, giving you a ready-to-use foundation for building customer relationship and service-tracking apps.

***

### Project Export and Prompt-with-AI Automation Actions

Two new automation actions are now available: **Project Export** — export a project's content on a trigger — and **Prompt with AI** — send a prompt to an AI model and use the response as a variable in downstream steps.

***

### Workspace DNA Interactive Flow Canvas (Phase 0)

Workspace DNA now includes an interactive flow canvas. Phase 0 lets you visualize and explore the structure of your workspace as a navigable graph.

***

### Guided Onboarding

A new guided onboarding experience walks new users through core Taskade concepts and first-time setup steps.

***

## Improvements & Fixes:

* General stability, performance, and onboarding improvements.
