## What's New:

### Delegated Custom-Domain Validation

Custom-domain validation is now fully delegated — workspace owners can complete domain verification without needing direct access to DNS provider credentials. The updated flow guides you through each step and confirms ownership automatically.

***

### MiniMax M3 Model

MiniMax M3 is now available as a model option across agents and automations.

***

### Password Reset Revokes Existing Sessions

For improved account security, initiating a password reset now revokes all active sessions. Users must sign in again with the new password on all devices.

***
