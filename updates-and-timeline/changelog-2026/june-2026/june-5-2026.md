## What's New:

### Published Apps Are Private by Default

Newly published Genesis apps are now private by default. You must explicitly grant access after publishing, giving you tighter control over who can reach your app from the moment it goes live.

***

### Papaparse Available in Base App Templates

The `papaparse` library is now bundled in base app templates, enabling CSV parsing directly within your Genesis apps without any extra setup.
