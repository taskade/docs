# January 28, 2026

## What's New:

### New Publish Flow

Your new apps will now default to "Secret." You can change their setting to "Public" if you wish to share them. Additionally, you can toggle "Share as Template" to allow other users to clone your apps.

### New Community "New" Tab

With the changes to the publishing flow, all "Public" apps will now be shown in the new Community "New" tab. Check out what everyone else is making!

## Improvements & Fixes:

* New 404 screens.
* New headers and footers for publicly embedded projects.
* You can now toggle to show or hide your side panel in the /settings page.
* Fixed an issue where some notifications in Activity was showing up as blank.
* Fixed an issue where long workspace names would squish the workspace icon.
