## What's New:

### Media Browse Tab

We've introduced a new way for you to browse and organise your Taskade media. You can now create folders and move your files between them at any depth.

### Auto Top-Up Credits

You can now enable Auto Top-Up in your[ Settings → Usage page](https://www.taskade.com/settings/usage) by toggling it on. When your credits run low, they will be automatically topped up if you have a payment method linked to your Taskade account.
