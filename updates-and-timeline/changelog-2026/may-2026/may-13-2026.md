## What's New:

### App Clone Ancestry Tracking

Taskade now tracks the lineage of cloned apps. When a paid app is cloned, the clone's ancestry is recorded so that re-sharing the clone as a new paid app is blocked. This protects creators' published work from being redistributed without authorization.
