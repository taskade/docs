## What's New:

### Shopify Connector — OAuth2

The Shopify integration ships an OAuth2 connector you can wire directly into automation flows and agent knowledge bases — no manual API-key handling required. Authorize once and Taskade manages token refresh automatically.

The connector source is open source in the [Integration Kit](https://github.com/taskade/integrations); see also [Integrations](../../../genesis-living-system-builder/automation/integrations.md).
