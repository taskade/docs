## What's New:

### Paid Apps — Sell Published Genesis Apps via Stripe

Genesis app creators are rolling out the ability to monetize their published apps. Stripe integration is available to enable paid access to apps you publish to the Taskade community. Buyers pay through Stripe; access is granted automatically upon successful payment. Configure pricing and connect a Stripe account from your app's publishing settings.
