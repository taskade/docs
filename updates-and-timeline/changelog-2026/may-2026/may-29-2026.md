## What's New:

### Delegated DCV for Custom Domains

Custom domain verification now uses Delegated Domain Control Validation (DCV). This requires fewer DNS records to maintain and speeds up HTTPS certificate provisioning, reducing the time between domain configuration and a live HTTPS endpoint.

### New Models Added — Claude Opus 4.8, Xiaomi Mimo, and More

Claude Opus 4.8, Xiaomi Mimo, and additional models are now available in the model picker for agents and automations.
