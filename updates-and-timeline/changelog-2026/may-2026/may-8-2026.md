## What's New:

### BYOK Verify-on-Save

Bring Your Own Key entries are now validated at the moment they are saved. If a key is invalid or the target provider rejects it, you receive an immediate error rather than discovering the problem later during an agent run. This applies to all BYOK-supported plan tiers.
