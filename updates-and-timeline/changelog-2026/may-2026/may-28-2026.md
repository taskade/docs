## What's New:

### Create Automations Directly from Agents, Teams, and Media

You can now create automation workflows directly from the Agents, Teams, and Media sections of the app, without navigating to the Automations hub first. This reduces friction when wiring up automations that are tightly coupled to a specific agent or team.

### App Categories for Published Apps

Published Genesis apps are now assigned to categories in the community marketplace. Categories improve discoverability for users browsing published apps.

### JSON-LD Structured Data for Published Apps

Published apps now emit JSON-LD structured data on their public-facing pages. This improves how search engines understand and index app content, benefiting SEO for publicly shared apps.
