### New Telegram Bot and Shopify Triggers

We've added a bunch of new triggers in automations for the Telegram Bot and Shopify.&#x20;

For **Telegram Bot**, you can now trigger an automation when the bot receives a new message, when a new member joins a group, or when the bot receives a callback query.

For **Shopify**, you can now trigger an automation when various new items are created, such as New Order, New Customer and more.&#x20;
